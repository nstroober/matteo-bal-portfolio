## Quick Tips

1. **Start with the header image** - This is the most important for first impression
2. **Use consistent naming** - Keep the exact filenames as shown above
3. **Optimize before uploading** - Compress images to 200-500KB each
4. **Test as you go** - Add a few images and test in browser before adding all

## Image Specifications

| Location | Recommended Size | Aspect Ratio | Format |
|----------|-----------------|--------------|--------|
| Header | 2400x1600px | 3:2 | JPG |
| Grid Images | 1200x1500px | 4:5 | JPG |
| Full-Width | 2400x1200px | 2:1 | JPG |
